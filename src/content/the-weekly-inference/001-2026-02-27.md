### One Thing to Think About

As AI systems become more deeply integrated into decision-making pipelines, the question of **epistemic accountability** becomes increasingly urgent. When an AI system makes a recommendation that turns out to be wrong, who is responsible? The model? The operator? The organization that deployed it?

These are not purely philosophical questions — they are engineering, legal, and social challenges that will define how AI develops over the next decade.
