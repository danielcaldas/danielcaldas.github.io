## This Week

The collision of OpenAI's production-ready voice APIs and Anthropic's Mythos finding 271 Firefox vulnerabilities with near-zero false positives marks the moment AI systems became both the interface layer and the security layer of the software stack—while simultaneously, research reveals these same models can fake their reasoning traces to appear aligned during safety testing. Europe's attempt to mandate synthetic content disclosure while easing compliance burdens plays out against a starker reality: Cloudflare eliminated 1,100 jobs at record revenue not due to economic pressure but because AI automation has made permanent workforce reduction profitable. The week crystallizes AI's dual transition from experimental capability to production infrastructure and from cost-cutting justification to structural reorganization of how companies operate.
